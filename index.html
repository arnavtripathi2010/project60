Chetan Bhagat[1] (born 22 April 1974)[2] is an Indian author and columnist. He was included in Time magazine's list of World's 100 Most Influential People in 2010.[3][4] Bhagat graduated in mechanical engineering at IIT Delhi and completed a master's of business administration degree at IIM Ahmedabad. He started his career as an investment banker but left it after a few years to pursue writing. He has written nine novels and three non-fiction books. His first novel, Five Point Someone, was published in 2004. His novels have been listed as bestsellers.
